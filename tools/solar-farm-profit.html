Understanding Solar Farm Investments

Key Formulas

Annual Energy Output (MWh):
System Capacity × Solar Irradiance × Efficiency × 365 ÷ 1000

Annual Revenue:
Energy Output × Electricity Price

NPV:
Σ (Cash Flow₍ₜ₎ ÷ (1 + discount rate)ᵗ)

ROI:
(Net Profit ÷ Total Investment) × 100

Important Factors

  • • Solar Irradiance: Amount of solar energy received per area per year
  • • System Efficiency: How well panels convert sunlight to electricity
  • • Degradation Rate: Annual decrease in panel efficiency
  • • O&M Costs: Operations and maintenance expenses
  • • Power Purchase Agreement: Contract terms affect revenue
  • • Tax Incentives: Can significantly improve returns

Note: This calculator provides estimates based on simplified assumptions. Actual solar farm investments involve complex factors including grid connection costs, land lease agreements, regulatory requirements, and variable electricity prices. Consult with solar energy professionals for detailed feasibility analysis.
