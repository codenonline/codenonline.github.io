Formula and Explanation

Spending Multiplier Formula

Multiplier = 1 / (1 - MPC) = 1 / MPS

Where MPC = Marginal Propensity to Consume, MPS = Marginal Propensity to Save

Key Concepts

  • MPC: Fraction of additional income spent
  • MPS: Fraction of additional income saved
  • Multiplier: Total economic impact ratio
  • Rounds: Successive spending cycles
