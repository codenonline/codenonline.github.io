Formula

Add Tax to Net Amount:

Gross Amount = Net Amount × (1 + Tax Rate) + Additional Fees

Tax-Inclusive Calculation:

Gross Amount = Net Amount ÷ (1 - Tax Rate) + Additional Fees

Usage Tips

  • Use "Add tax" when you know the net price and want to add tax on top
  • Use "Tax-inclusive" when the net amount already includes tax
  • Add additional fees for handling charges, service fees, etc.
  • Use quick presets for common tax rates in different regions
