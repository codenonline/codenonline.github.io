Formula & Education

Formula

Retention Ratio = (Net Income - Dividends) ÷ Net Income
OR
Retention Ratio = 1 - Dividend Payout Ratio

Key Terms

Net Income: Company's total profit after taxes
Dividends: Cash payments made to shareholders
Retained Earnings: Profits kept in the company for reinvestment

Interpretation Guidelines

Low (0-30%): High dividend payments, limited reinvestment
Moderate (30-70%): Balanced approach to dividends and growth
High (70-100%): Focus on reinvestment and growth

Usage Tips

  • • Compare with industry averages
  • • Consider company growth stage
  • • Analyze alongside growth metrics
  • • Look at historical trends
  • • Consider shareholder preferences
