DSO Formula

DSO = (Accounts Receivable ÷ Total Credit Sales) × Number of Days

Accounts Receivable

The total amount owed by customers for goods or services sold on credit.

Total Credit Sales

The total sales made on credit during the specified time period.

Number of Days

The time period being analyzed (typically 365 days for annual DSO).

Tips to Improve DSO

Collection Strategies

  • Send invoices immediately upon delivery
  • Implement automated payment reminders
  • Offer early payment discounts
  • Conduct credit checks on new customers

Process Improvements

  • Streamline invoicing processes
  • Set clear payment terms
  • Monitor aging reports regularly
  • Train staff on collection procedures
