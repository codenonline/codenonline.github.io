Understanding Actual Cash Value

What is ACV?

Actual Cash Value is the amount equal to the replacement cost minus depreciation of a damaged or stolen property at the time of the loss.

ACV Formula:

ACV = Replacement Cost - Depreciation

Depreciation Methods:

  • Straight-Line: Equal depreciation each year
  • Declining Balance: Higher depreciation in early years
  • Custom Rate: Specific depreciation rate per year

Typical Useful Life by Category:

Electronics: 3-7 years
Furniture: 10-15 years
Appliances: 8-15 years
Vehicles: 10-20 years
Jewelry: 20+ years
Clothing: 3-5 years
Tools: 5-15 years

Tips for Accurate ACV:

  • Research current replacement costs
  • Consider item condition honestly
  • Account for wear and tear
  • Include all relevant receipts
  • Consider market depreciation rates
