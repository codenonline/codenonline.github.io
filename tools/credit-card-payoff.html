💡 Strategy Recommendations

✅ Debt Avalanche is mathematically optimal

You'll save in interest by focusing on highest APR cards first.

✅ Debt Snowball provides psychological wins

While it costs more, you'll see progress faster with smaller balances paid off first.

⚖️ Both methods are very similar

The difference is minimal, so choose the method that motivates you most.

Debt Payoff Tips

Debt Avalanche Benefits:

  • • Mathematically optimal - saves the most money
  • • Reduces total interest paid
  • • Best for disciplined payers
  • • Focus on highest APR cards first

Debt Snowball Benefits:

  • • Provides quick psychological wins
  • • Builds momentum and motivation
  • • Simplifies your debt portfolio faster
  • • Focus on smallest balances first

General Tips:

  • • Stop using credit cards while paying off debt
  • • Consider balance transfers to lower APR cards
  • • Look for extra income sources to increase payments
  • • Automate payments to stay consistent
  • • Celebrate milestones to stay motivated
