How It's Calculated

Customer Retention Rate Formula

Retention Rate = ((End Customers - New Customers) / Start Customers) × 100

Key Terms

  • Start Customers: Number of customers at the beginning of the period
  • End Customers: Number of customers at the end of the period
  • New Customers: Number of customers acquired during the period
  • Retained Customers: End customers minus new customers
  • Churn Rate: Percentage of customers lost (100% - Retention Rate)

Tips for Improving Retention

Best Practices:

  • Provide excellent customer service
  • Regular customer feedback collection
  • Implement loyalty programs
  • Personalize customer experiences

Warning Signs:

  • Declining retention rates over time
  • High customer acquisition costs
  • Increased customer complaints
  • Longer response times to issues
