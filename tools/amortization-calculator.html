Formula & Tips

Payment Formula

PMT = P × [r(1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n - 1]

Where P = Principal, r = periodic rate, n = number of payments

Key Benefits

  • • See exactly how much goes to principal vs interest
  • • Plan for early payoff strategies
  • • Compare different loan terms
  • • Understand total cost of borrowing

Tips

  • • Early payments go mostly to interest
  • • Later payments go mostly to principal
  • • Extra principal payments reduce total interest
  • • Shorter terms mean higher payments but less interest
