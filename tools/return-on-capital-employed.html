Understanding ROCE

Formula

ROCE = (EBIT ÷ Capital Employed) × 100

What it Measures

  • Efficiency of capital utilization
  • Management's ability to generate profits
  • Business profitability and performance
  • Long-term sustainability potential

Interpretation Guidelines

Excellent (>20%)
Very efficient use of capital
Good (15-20%)
Above average performance
Average (10-15%)
Industry standard performance
Poor (<10%)
Inefficient capital utilization

Tips for Improving ROCE

Increase Profitability

  • Improve operational efficiency
  • Reduce operating costs
  • Increase pricing margins
  • Focus on high-margin products

Optimize Capital Usage

  • Reduce excess inventory
  • Optimize asset utilization
  • Improve working capital management
  • Divest non-performing assets
