Understanding Revenue Calculation

Basic Revenue Formula

Revenue = Units Sold × Price per Unit

  • Revenue is the total income from sales
  • Also called gross revenue or turnover
  • Does not account for costs or expenses
  • Key metric for business performance

Revenue Types

  • Product Revenue: Income from selling goods
  • Service Revenue: Income from providing services
  • Recurring Revenue: Ongoing income (subscriptions)
  • One-time Revenue: Single transaction income

Key Considerations

Revenue Recognition

Record revenue when earned, not necessarily when cash is received

Revenue vs Profit

Revenue is before expenses; profit is after subtracting all costs
