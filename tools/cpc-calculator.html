CPC Formula

Cost Per Click (CPC) =

Total Cost ÷ Total Clicks

Additional Formulas:

• Total Cost = CPC × Total Clicks

• Total Clicks = Total Cost ÷ CPC

• CTR = (Clicks ÷ Impressions) × 100

• CPA = Total Cost ÷ Conversions

• ROAS = Revenue ÷ Total Cost

Optimization Tips

  • • Target long-tail keywords for lower CPC
  • • Improve ad quality score to reduce costs
  • • Use negative keywords to avoid irrelevant clicks
  • • Optimize landing pages for better conversion rates
  • • Test different ad copy and targeting options
  • • Monitor competitor strategies and adjust bids
