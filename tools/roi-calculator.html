Understanding ROI

ROI Interpretation

  • • Positive ROI: Investment generated a profit
  • • Negative ROI: Investment resulted in a loss
  • • 0% ROI: Investment broke even
  • • Higher ROI: Better investment performance

Formula Used

Simple ROI = ((Final Value - Total Investment) / Total Investment) × 100
Annualized ROI = (((Final Value / Total Investment)^(1/Years)) - 1) × 100

Total Investment = Initial Investment + Additional Costs
