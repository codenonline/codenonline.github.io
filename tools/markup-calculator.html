Markup vs Margin

Markup:

  • • Markup = (Selling Price - Cost) ÷ Cost × 100
  • • Selling Price = Cost × (1 + Markup%)
  • • Based on cost price
  • • Can exceed 100%

Profit Margin:

  • • Margin = (Selling Price - Cost) ÷ Selling Price × 100
  • • Based on selling price
  • • Cannot exceed 100%
  • • 50% markup = 33.3% margin
