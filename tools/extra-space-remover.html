How it Works

What gets removed:

  • • Multiple consecutive spaces
  • • Leading spaces at the beginning of lines
  • • Trailing spaces at the end of lines
  • • Tab characters (when selected)
  • • Empty lines (when selected)
  • • Mixed whitespace characters

Options explained:

  • • Normalize: Standardizes all whitespace
  • • Preserve single spaces: Keeps intentional spacing
  • • Tab conversion: Replaces tabs with spaces
  • • Trim lines: Removes leading/trailing spaces
  • • Remove empty lines: Eliminates blank lines
