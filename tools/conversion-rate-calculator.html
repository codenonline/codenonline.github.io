Formula Reference

Basic Conversion Rate

Conversion Rate = (Conversions ÷ Visitors) × 100

A/B Test Statistical Significance

Uses Z-test for proportion comparison with 95% confidence level

Funnel Drop-off Rate

Drop-off = ((Previous Step - Current Step) ÷ Previous Step) × 100

Revenue Impact

Revenue = Visitors × Conversion Rate × Average Order Value

Improvement Calculation

Improvement = ((New Rate - Old Rate) ÷ Old Rate) × 100

Confidence Interval

CI = Rate ± Z × √(Rate × (1 - Rate) ÷ Sample Size)

Conversion Rate Optimization Tips

Testing Best Practices

  • • Test one element at a time for clear results
