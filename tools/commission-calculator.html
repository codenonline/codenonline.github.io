Understanding Commission Structures

Commission Types

Flat Rate: Simple percentage of all sales, easy to understand and calculate.

Tiered: Different rates for different sales levels, rewards higher performance.

Graduated: Rate increases gradually with volume, provides continuous incentive.

Draw: Advance payment against future commissions, provides income stability.

Choosing the Right Structure

Consider: Sales cycle length, average deal size, team experience level

Goals: Motivate specific behaviors, retain top performers, attract new talent

Balance: Company profitability with competitive compensation

Best Practices

  • • Set clear, achievable targets
  • • Align with business objectives
  • • Regular performance reviews
  • • Transparent calculation methods
  • • Consider market competitiveness

Common Pitfalls

  • • Overly complex structures
  • • Unrealistic targets
  • • Ignoring team collaboration
  • • Infrequent plan updates
  • • Poor communication
