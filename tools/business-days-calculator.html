Understanding Business Days

What are Business Days?

Business days are weekdays (Monday through Friday) when most businesses operate. They exclude weekends (Saturday and Sunday) and public holidays.

Common Uses

  • Project planning and timeline estimation
  • Delivery and shipping calculations
  • Contract and legal deadlines
  • Banking and financial transactions
  • Service level agreements (SLAs)

Important Notes

  • Business days vary by country and region
  • Public holidays differ between countries
  • Some businesses may operate on different schedules
  • Always verify specific business hours with relevant parties
