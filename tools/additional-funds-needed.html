Interpretation

AFN Formula & Guide

Formula

AFN = (A/S₀) × ΔS - (L/S₀) × ΔS - MS₁ × RR

Where:

A/S₀ = Asset-to-sales ratio

ΔS = Change in sales

L/S₀ = Spontaneous liability-to-sales ratio

M = Profit margin

S₁ = Projected sales

RR = Retention ratio

Key Concepts

Spontaneous Liabilities:

Liabilities that increase automatically with sales (accounts payable, accrued expenses).

Retention Ratio:

Percentage of net income retained in the business rather than paid as dividends.

Asset Intensity:

How much assets are required to support each dollar of sales.

Tips for Reducing AFN

Operational Strategies

  • Improve asset turnover ratios
  • Reduce inventory levels
  • Speed up collections
  • Extend payment terms with suppliers

Financial Strategies

  • Increase profit margins
  • Retain more earnings (lower dividends)
  • Use operating leases instead of purchases
  • Optimize capital structure
