How It Works

Formula

Adjusted Salary = Current Salary × (1 + Inflation Rate)^Number of Years

Key Concepts

  • • Purchasing Power: The amount of goods and services your salary can buy
  • • Inflation: The general increase in prices over time, reducing purchasing power
  • • Real vs Nominal: Nominal salary is the dollar amount; real salary is adjusted for inflation
  • • Compound Effect: Inflation compounds annually, meaning its impact grows over time

Usage Tips

  • • Use this calculator for salary negotiations and career planning
  • • Consider regional differences in inflation rates
  • • Historical US inflation averages 2-4% annually
  • • Account for industry-specific inflation and cost-of-living changes
