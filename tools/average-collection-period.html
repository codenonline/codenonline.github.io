Understanding Average Collection Period

Formula

Average Collection Period =
Average Accounts Receivable
────────────────────────
Daily Credit Sales
Where Daily Sales = Annual Credit Sales ÷ Period Days

Alternative Formula

ACP = Period Days ÷ Receivables Turnover

Key Insights

  • Measures efficiency in collecting receivables
  • Lower periods indicate faster collection
  • Should be compared to payment terms
  • Helps assess credit and collection policies
  • Important for cash flow management
