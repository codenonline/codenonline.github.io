Understanding BMI

BMI Categories:

  • • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • • Normal weight: BMI 18.5-24.9
  • • Overweight: BMI 25-29.9
  • • Obese: BMI 30 or greater

Important Notes:

  • • BMI doesn't measure body fat directly
  • • May not be accurate for athletes
  • • Consult healthcare provider for advice
  • • Consider other health factors
