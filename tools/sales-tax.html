Sales Tax Formulas

Adding Sales Tax:

  • • Sales Tax = Price × (Tax Rate ÷ 100)
  • • Total Price = Price + Sales Tax
  • • Or: Total Price = Price × (1 + Tax Rate ÷ 100)

Removing Sales Tax:

  • • Price Before Tax = Total Price ÷ (1 + Tax Rate ÷ 100)
  • • Sales Tax = Total Price - Price Before Tax
  • • Tax Rate = (Sales Tax ÷ Price Before Tax) × 100
