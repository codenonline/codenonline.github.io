Formulas & Information

Labor Productivity

Measures the economic output per unit of labor input.

Labor Productivity = Total Output / Total Labor Hours
Output per Employee = Total Output / Number of Employees

Revenue per Employee

Measures how much revenue each employee generates on average.

Revenue per Employee = Total Revenue / Number of Employees

Task Efficiency

Measures completion rate, speed, and time management effectiveness.

Completion Rate = (Tasks Completed / Total Tasks) × 100
Time Efficiency = (Estimated Time / Actual Time) × 100

Manufacturing Efficiency

Measures production efficiency, quality rate, and overall equipment effectiveness.

Production Efficiency = (Units Produced / Max Possible Units) × 100
Quality Rate = ((Units Produced - Defective Units) / Units Produced) × 100
