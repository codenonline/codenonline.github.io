About CPM

CPM (Cost Per Mille) measures the cost of 1,000 advertising impressions. It is a standard metric for comparing ad campaign efficiency across different channels.

Formula

CPM = (Total Cost / Impressions) x 1,000

Lower CPM indicates more cost-efficient ad spending. Industry averages vary widely by platform and format.
