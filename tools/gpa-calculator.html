Tips & Information

How GPA is Calculated

  • • Quality Points = Grade Points × Credit Hours
  • • GPA = Total Quality Points ÷ Total Credit Hours
  • • Each course contributes based on its credit weight
  • • Higher credit courses have more impact on GPA

GPA Improvement Tips

  • • Focus on courses with higher credit hours
  • • Retake courses where allowed to replace grades
  • • Consider course load vs. grade quality balance
  • • Use grade forgiveness policies when available
