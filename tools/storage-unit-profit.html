Storage Unit Investment Guide

Key Metrics Explained

  • Cash-on-Cash Return: Annual cash flow ÷ Total cash invested
  • Cap Rate: Net Operating Income ÷ Property Value
  • Cash Flow: Net income minus all expenses including debt service
  • Occupancy Rate: Percentage of units that are rented
  • NOI: Net Operating Income before debt service

Investment Tips

  • • Target occupancy rates of 85-95% in established markets
  • • Budget 10-15% of gross income for maintenance
  • • Consider climate-controlled units for higher rents
  • • Location near residential areas is crucial
  • • Factor in competition from nearby facilities
  • • Plan for seasonal occupancy variations
