How Email Extraction Works

Pattern Matching: Uses regex patterns to identify email addresses in text

Validation: Checks email format against RFC standards

Deduplication: Removes duplicate email addresses from results

Domain Analysis: Groups emails by domain for analysis

Supported Formats:

• Standard format: user@domain.com

• With display names: "John Doe" &lt;john@example.com&gt;

• Subdomain emails: user@mail.example.com

• International domains: user@example.co.uk
