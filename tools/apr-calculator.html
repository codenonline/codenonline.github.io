Understanding APR

What's Included in APR:

  • • Base interest rate
  • • Origination fees
  • • Points paid to lender
  • • Mortgage insurance premiums
  • • Processing fees

What's NOT Included:

  • • Appraisal fees
  • • Credit report fees
  • • Title insurance
  • • Attorney fees
  • • Inspection fees
