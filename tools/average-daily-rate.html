How ADR is Calculated

ADR = Total Room Revenue ÷ Rooms Sold

Key Components:

  • Total Room Revenue: All revenue from room sales
  • Rooms Sold: Number of occupied rooms
  • RevPAR: ADR × Occupancy Rate
  • Occupancy Rate: (Rooms Sold ÷ Available Rooms) × 100

Tips for Improving ADR

Revenue Management

Use dynamic pricing based on demand patterns

Room Upgrades

Offer premium rooms and upsell services

Adjust rates for peak and off-peak periods
